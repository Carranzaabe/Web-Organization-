Attire & Uniforms

Laboratory Attire

For all laboratory sessions, students must adhere to the following laboratory attire requirements for safety and professional purposes:

  • Laboratory Coat: White, full-length laboratory coat with ILS patch sewn on the left sleeve.
  • Safety Goggles: Clear safety goggles that provide adequate eye protection during experiments.
  • Gloves: Disposable nitrile or latex gloves appropriate for the laboratory work being conducted.
  • Closed-toe Shoes: Leather or synthetic material shoes that fully cover the foot. No sandals, mesh shoes, or high heels allowed.
  • Hair and Accessories: Long hair must be tied back. Minimal jewelry is permitted for safety reasons.

Important: Students without proper laboratory attire will not be permitted to participate in laboratory sessions.

Academic & Professional Attire

For regular class days and formal events, students are expected to maintain a neat and professional appearance:

Regular Class Days

  • Male Students: Collared shirts, slacks or neat jeans, closed shoes. No shorts, sleeveless shirts, or sandals.
  • Female Students: Blouses, slacks, skirts of appropriate length, closed shoes. No extremely short skirts, revealing tops, or sandals.

Note: For conferences and external events, business attire is required unless otherwise specified.
