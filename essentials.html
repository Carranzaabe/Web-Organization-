First Year Essentials

Everything you need to know to succeed in your first year as a Biology student at PUP Lopez Campus. We've compiled the essential resources to help you excel on your academic journey.

Laboratory Equipment

Essential tools and equipment you'll need for your laboratory classes to ensure successful experiments and practical learning.

Microscopy Tools

  • Microscope slide box (50 pcs)
  • Cover slips (100 pcs)
  • Lens cleaning paper
  • Dropper bottles for stains
  • Slide preparation kit

Safety Equipment

  • Laboratory coat (white, long sleeves)
  • Safety goggles
  • Latex gloves (box of 100)
  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Hair ties (for long hair)

Dissection Tools

  • Dissection kit (scissors, probes, forceps)
  • Scalpel with replacement blades
  • Dissection tray
  • Specimen storage containers
  • Disposable aprons

First Year Course Schedule

Typical first-year subjects for BS Biology students to help you plan your academic year effectively.

First Semester

  • General Biology I with Laboratory
  • General Chemistry I with Laboratory
  • Mathematics in the Modern World
  • Purposive Communication
  • Understanding the Self
  • Physical Education I

Second Semester

  • General Biology II with Laboratory
  • General Chemistry II with Laboratory
  • Ethics
  • Science, Technology, and Society
  • Readings in Philippine History
  • Physical Education II
