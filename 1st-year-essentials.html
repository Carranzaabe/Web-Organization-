1st Year Essentials

Everything you need to know and have for your first year as a Biology student at PUP Lopez Campus.

Laboratory Equipment Checklist

Essential tools and equipment you'll need for your laboratory classes.

Microscopy Tools

  • Microscope slide box (50 pcs)
  • Cover slips (100 pcs)
  • Lens cleaning paper
  • Dropper bottles for stains (3 pcs)
  • Slide preparation kit
  • Laboratory notebook with gridlines
  • Drawing pencils (HB, 2B)

Safety Equipment

  • Laboratory coat (white, long sleeves)
  • Safety goggles (clear, anti-fog)
  • Latex gloves (box of 100)
  • Face mask (box of 50)
  • Closed-toe shoes (non-slip)
  • Hair ties (for long hair)
  • First aid kit (small, personal)

Dissection Tools

  • Dissection kit (scissors, probes, forceps)
  • Scalpel with replacement blades
  • Dissection tray (with wax bottom)
  • Specimen pins (box of 100)
  • Specimen storage containers (3 pcs)
  • Disposable aprons (pack of 10)
  • Fine-pointed scissors

Essential Textbooks & Resources

Recommended reading materials and digital resources for your first year courses.

Core Textbooks

Campbell Biology (12th Edition)

Authors: Lisa A. Urry, Michael L. Cain, Steven A. Wasserman, et al.

Publisher: Pearson

Required for: General Biology I & II

Human Anatomy & Physiology (12th Edition)

Authors: Elaine N. Marieb, Katja Hoehn

Publisher: Pearson

Required for: Human Anatomy & Physiology

Principles of Chemistry (3rd Edition)

Authors: Nivaldo J. Tro

Publisher: Pearson

Required for: General Chemistry I & II

Digital Resources

JSTOR & PubMed Access

Access to scientific journals and research papers. Login credentials will be provided during orientation.

Virtual Lab Simulations

Online platform for practicing lab techniques and experiments. Subscription provided by the department.

Mobile Apps

Recommended apps include Cell Biology 3D, Anatomy 3D Atlas, and Chemistry AR. Available for both iOS and Android.

Where to Purchase

Recommended locations to purchase your equipment and textbooks.

On-Campus Resources

  • Campus Bookstore - Located at the Student Center, Ground Floor
  • Biology Department Store - Science Building, Room 103
  • University Co-op - Main Building, East Wing

All on-campus stores offer student discounts with valid ID.

Off-Campus Options

  • Scientific Supply Store - Located 2 blocks from campus
  • Medical Supply Center - Downtown, offers student discounts
  • Online Retailers - Amazon, LabSupplies.ph, ScienceKits.com

Check with senior students for recommendations on the best deals.

Need Financial Assistance?

We offer scholarships and financial aid options to help with your educational expenses. The Biology Department also has a limited equipment loan program for eligible students.
