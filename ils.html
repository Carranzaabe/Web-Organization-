Mission

The program is committed to providing a strong foundation in biological principles, research methodologies, and critical thinking to prepare graduates for diverse careers in healthcare, biotechnology, environmental conservation, and scientific research. It strives to cultivate a culture of ethical scientific inquiry, sustainability, and lifelong learning while encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration and real-world application of biological sciences for the betterment of society.

Vision

To be a leading institution in biological sciences, fostering innovation, research, and environmental stewardship to address global challenges and advance scientific knowledge for the betterment of society.
